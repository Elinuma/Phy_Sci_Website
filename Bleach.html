Description

         This liquid is designed specifically to whiten various the surfaces.
         It works by using its chemicals to clean and remove dirt,grease whilst
         also whitening it.

How to Use

            1. For cleaning surfaces, dilute with water (usually 1 cup of bleach per gallon of water).
            2. Apply to the surface using a cloth or spray bottle.
            3. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes, then rinse with clean water.

General Ingredients

                Sodium Hypochlorite,Water,Sodium Hydroxide,Surfactants,
                Stabilizers

Precautions

        Keep out of eyes,keep out of children's reach,follow manufacturer's instructions
        Follow local guidelines on hazardous waste disposal.
