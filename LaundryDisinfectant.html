Description

            Laundry Disinfectants are specialized products used to kill bacteria, viruses, 
            and other germs on clothes and fabrics during the washing process.

How to Use

            1. Follow the instructions on the label for the correct dosage.
            2. Add to the washing machine along with your regular detergent.
            3. Wash according to fabric care instructions.

General Ingredients

            Quaternary Ammonium Compounds (Quats),Sodium Hypochlorite,Hydrogen Peroxide
            Surfactants,Fragrance,Colorants,Stabilizers,Preservatives,Water.

Precautions

        Keep out of eyes,keep out of children's reach,follow manufacturer's instructions.
        Follow local guidelines on hazardous waste disposal.
