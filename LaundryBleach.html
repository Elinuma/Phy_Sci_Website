Laundry Bleach

₱20 to ₱250 (Depending on brand)

Description

            Laundry Bleach is a chemical solution specifically formulated to whiten clothes, 
            remove stains, and disinfect fabrics during the washing process.

How to Use

            1. Dilute with water according to the instructions (usually ¾ cup per gallon of water).
            2. Add to the bleach dispenser in the washing machine or soak whites in a solution.
            3. Do not use on colored fabrics unless specified.

General Ingredients

            Sodium Hypochlorite,Water,Sodium Hydroxide,Surfactants,
            Stabilizers,Fragrance,Colorants.

Precautions

        Keep out of eyes,keep out of children's reach,follow manufacturer's instructions.
        Follow local guidelines on hazardous waste disposal.
