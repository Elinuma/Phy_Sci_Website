Description

            Dishwashing Liquid is designed specifically for cleaning dishes, utensils, 
            and cookware. It works by breaking down grease, food residue, and stains, making it easier to wash them off.

How to Use

         1. Fill a sink or basin with warm water.
         2. Add a few drops of dishwashing liquid.
         3. Use a sponge or cloth to scrub dishes, pots, and pans.
         4. Rinse thoroughly with clean water.
