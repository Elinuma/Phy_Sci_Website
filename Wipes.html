Wipes

₱15 to ₱50 (Depending on brand)

Description

            Disinfectant Wipes are pre-dampened cloth containing an active solution 
            that is intended to kill and prevent bacteria from spreading.

How to Use

            1. Open the container and pull out a wipe.
            2. Wipe the surface until visibly wet.
            3. Allow the surface to air dry.

General Ingredients

            Quaternary Ammonium Compounds (Quats),Sodium Hypochlorite,Hydrogen Peroxide
            Surfactants,Fragrance,Colorants,Stabilizers,Preservatives,Water,Thickening Agent
            pH Adjusters.

Precautions

        Avoid flushing down the toilet,follow manufacturer's instructions.
        Follow local guidelines on hazardous waste disposal.
